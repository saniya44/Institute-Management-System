About us

"Karale Computer Typing Institute"
Principal: Mr. Prakash Bhimraj Karale

    "Karale Computer Typing Institute is one of the best coaching institute in Ahmedanagar. This institute has started in year 1991 and have completed successfull 25 years with hundreds of students having well known government and private jobs. Our Institute provide many courses such as computer typing English, Hindi, Marathi, foreign languages courses, Tally Dtp courses, and many more. Institute recently have also started the mpsc courses for the students appearing for the mpsc exams. Institute's principal Mr. Prakash Karale strives hard to provide high level education to the students and to secure their lives. Institute wish to keep providing the knowledge to the students and to make them capable to cope up with today's world."
